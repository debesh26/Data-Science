
Family                         The estimated extent to which Family contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score.
	  	Equaivalent  to “If you are in trouble ,  do you have relatives or friends you can count on to help you ,  or not?” 

GDP per Capita          The estimated extent to which GDP per Capita contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score.
	

Health 		The estimated extent to which Health (Life Expectancy) contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score. 

Freedom 		The estimated extent to which Freedom contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score.
		Equivalent to  “Are you satisfied or dissatisfied with your freedom to choose what you do with your life?”

 Generosity 	The estimated extent to which Generosity contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score.
		Equivalent to  “Have you donated money to a charity in the past month?”


 Trust 		The estimated extent to which Trust contributes to the calculation of the Happiness Score.
		Equivalent to : “Is corruption widespread throughout the government or not?”  &   “Is corruption widespread within businesses or not?”

Dystopia Residual     Each country is compared against a hypothetical nation called Dystopia. 
		Dystopia represents the lowest national average for each of the six key variables 
		Hence Dystopia is used as a benchmark for regression.
